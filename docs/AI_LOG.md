### Colour palette
Brainstormed colour options with AI. After having checked electronic stores in Norway: Power, Komplett, Elkjøp and Kjell & Company, and looking at a Phycology of Colour chart, I decided that a shade of blue will be my primary colour. AI suggested using a bright orange or electric green as my accent colour. I asked it if grey would work as well. Got a response saying that it works well for text, borders and secondary text. I think black for body text is a safer bet to ensure good contrast on white background.

Final suggestion output: "Go with blue primary + grey secondary + vibrant accent. Use:
* Blue for headers, navigation, links
* Grey for text, borders, subtle elements
* A vibrant colour (orange or coral) ONLY for important actions like "Add to Cart" buttons".
